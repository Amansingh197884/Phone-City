About Developer

A real estate developer is an entrepreneur who manages the entire lifecycle of property projects—from acquiring land and securing financing to overseeing design, construction, and sales. They lead teams of architects, contractors, and marketers to build residential or commercial spaces, playing a pivotal role in urban development.

1. Core Functions:

They identify profitable opportunities, conduct feasibility studies, and manage the risk involved in transforming land into valuable assets.

2. Key Responsibilities:

Beyond building, they are responsible for navigating regulatory approvals, securing funding, and implementing marketing strategies to sell or lease projects.
